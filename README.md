Amazon Laptop Sales Dashboard – Excel Project

Project Overview

The Amazon Laptop Sales Dashboard is an interactive and visually appealing analytical tool designed in Microsoft Excel to track and analyze laptop sales data from Amazon. This dashboard provides key insights into sales performance, revenue trends, customer preferences, and product demand.

Objective

The goal of this project is to enhance data-driven decision-making by visualizing crucial sales metrics, identifying trends, and improving business strategies based on data insights.

Key Features

Sales Performance Analysis – Tracks total sales, revenue, and profit trends.

Top-Selling Brands & Models – Identifies the best-selling laptop brands and models.

Monthly & Yearly Sales Trends – Displays sales fluctuations over different time periods.

Price vs. Sales Correlation – Analyzes how pricing affects laptop sales.

Customer Reviews & Ratings Impact – Evaluates how customer feedback influences purchases.

Market Share Representation – Highlights the contribution of different brands to total sales.


Tools & Techniques Used

Excel Pivot Tables & Charts – For dynamic data summarization.

Power Query – For efficient data cleaning and transformation.

Conditional Formatting – To highlight key trends and insights.

Data Validation & Filters – For interactive user-driven analysis.

Dashboards & Visualizations – To present data in an intuitive and user-friendly manner.


Outcome & Impact

This project enables stakeholders to gain quick and actionable insights into laptop sales trends, helping Amazon and sellers make informed decisions regarding pricing, inventory management, and marketing strategies.
